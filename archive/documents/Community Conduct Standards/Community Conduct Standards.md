<h1 align="center">
Community Conduct Standards
</h1>
<p align="center"><strong>for the Kingdom of Zatsunei and its Services</strong></p>


<div align="center">
<p>The Community Conduct Standards (CCS) establish rules for all people participating in games, services, or other activities within the Kingdom of Zatsunei. They define acceptable behavior, outline prohibited actions, and clarify expectations both in and out of roleplay. The CCS apply whenever conduct falls outside these standards or occurs out-of-character (OOC).
</p>

<strong><sup>THIS DOCUMENT IS CONSIDERED AN EXTERNAL REGULATION AND DOES NOT FALL UNDER THE CONTEXT OF ROLEPLAY OR ANY STANDARD PROCEDURES. THIS DOCUMENT IS ADMINISTERED SOLELY BY THE ADMINISTRATION OF ZATSUNEI.</sup></strong>

**Using loopholes to circumvent the rules and policies stated here will result in moderation.**

*The CCS may be updated any time at our discretion.*

<p>OOC = out-of-character: Any communication, action, or behavior that occurs outside the roleplay context.
Administration: People within the Kingdom of Zatsunei that are designated administrators or owners.</p>

        <summary><h2>Part I. General Guidelines</h2></summary>

### 1. Respectful Conduct
> - Treat all members with respect and consideration in all interactions.
> - Do not conduct in any form of discrimination or harassment, including but not limited to race, gender, sexual orientation, nationality, religion, illness, or other protected personal characteristics.
> 
> - Avoid bullying, intimidation, or out-of-character (OOC) verbal abuse.

### 2. Privacy and Security
> - Do not share personal information. Never publish real names, addresses, phone numbers, age, or other sensitive data without explicit consent.  
> - No doxxing. Sharing private information about others without permission is strictly prohibited, regardless of legal status in various countries.
>   
> - No DDoS or cyber attacks. Any attempt to disrupt servers or networks will result in immediate removal and reporting.  
> - Private information shared with you must remain private unless the owner explicitly allows sharing.
> - No malicious or deceptive technical activity. Any attempt to compromise systems, networks, accounts, or user privacy by technical means is prohibited.

### 3. Not Safe for Work / Life
> - Any form of sexual activity is strictly prohibited. This includes, but is not limited to, explicit content, inappropriate gestures, or any actions that are intended to be sexually suggestive or explicit.
>
> - Any behavior meant to incite sexual activity or discomfort is strictly forbidden. This includes actions that mimic or encourage inappropriate interactions, whether depicted as intentional or unintentional.
> - No sharing of explicit, graphic, or adult content. This applies to avatars, profiles, chat, or any other (in-game) interactions.
> - No inappropriate behavior in any form, whether itâ€™s verbal or physical. This includes unwanted advances, harassment, or suggestive actions towards other players.

### 4. Disruptive Behavior
> - Do not intentionally disrupt conversations or provoke arguments. 
>  
> - Spamming, flooding, or repeated off-topic messages is prohibited.  
> - Trolling, (rage)baiting, or any behavior intended to annoy or provoke others is not allowed.

### 5. Illegal Activity
> - Do not create, share, or distribute any material or messages that are illegal under applicable laws.  
>
> - Prohibited content includes, but is not limited to:
>   - Material involving the abuse, exploitation, or harm of children or animals.  
>   - Content depicting, promoting, or facilitating criminal activities.  
>   - Illicit goods, services, or instructions intended to enable unlawful acts.  
>   - OOC Acts of deception and fraud, such as scamming, spreading misinformation, impersonation, and defamation, libel, or slander.
>   - Any other content that violates criminal or civil law of real-world countries.

### 6. Advertisement
> - Do not promote, advertise, recruit, or post links for yourself, others, communities, products, games, or services without explicit permission from the administration.

### 7. Common Sense
> - Always think before you act and conduct yourself according to [common sense](https://simple.wikipedia.org/wiki/Common_sense) and reasonable judgment. 

### 8. Mini-Modding
> - Do not impersonate or act as a moderator or administrator if you are not one.
>
> - If you encounter rule-breaking content or behavior, report it to an official moderator or administrator.

### 9. Sensitive Topics
> - Refrain from initiating discussions or debates on politics, religion, or other sensitive subjects, including topics such as suicide, self-harm, terrorism, or controversial public figures. 
> 
> - If discussing sensitive topics, do so responsibly, with consideration for others, and avoid content that may cause division, distress, or conflict.
>
> - Avoid discussing personal thoughts of suicide or self-harm.  
If you or someone else is in crisis or at risk of self-harm or suicide, seek immediate help from a trained professional or contact a [helpline](https://findahelpline.com/).
>   - Threats of self-harm or suicide may lead to a permanent ban and will be reported to relevant authorites.

### 10. Online-Dating
> - Engaging in online dating within the Kingdom of Zatsunei is strictly prohibited.
> 
> - Do not participate in flirtatious, romantic, or suggestive behavior with other players.  
> - Sharing or requesting personal contact information for dating purposes is forbidden.

### 11. Service Agreements
> - You agree to comply with the terms, guidelines, and policies of any service used by the Kingdom of Zatsunei, including but not limited to Roblox, Discord, and Minecraft.
>   - You confirm that you are at least 13 years of age, and have obtained parental consent where required, to use any services that enforce such age restrictions.

    <details open>
        <summary><h2>Part II. Roleplay-related Guidelines</h2></summary>

### 1. Fail Roleplay (FRP)
> - Participation in roleplay is optional, but players are required to remain in character while actively involved in roleplay scenarios.
>   
> - If you choose not to engage in roleplay, you must clearly indicate this and leave any ongoing scenes. Starting or provoking new roleplay scenarios is not allowed in this case.  
> - Adapt your actions and decisions to the context of the scene, maintaining consistency and realism with your character and the situation.  
> - In-character actions, such as *takes* or *cuffs*, do not need to be explicitly narrated.

### 2. Alternate Accounts (Alts)
> - Alternate accounts are not allowed in any way. We do this to enforce one identity for one player and to prevent moderation evasion. Exceptions may be made upon explicit authorization by the administration.

### 3. Attention-seeking
> - Trolling or intentionally disrupting a roleplay scene, including actions such as cop-baiting, is considered disruptive behavior and falls under [Part I. Section 4.](#4-disruptive-behavior).

### 4. Leaving to Avoid Arrest / Action (LTTA/RTTA)
> - Leaving or resetting in-game to avoid arrest, detention, imprisonment, or moderation actions is prohibited.

### 5. Abusing Game Mechanics
> - Abusing game mechanics, such as exploiting glitches, bugs, or unintended features, is strictly prohibited. 
> 
> - Using modifications, software, or tools to gain an unfair advantage over others in any game is also prohibited.  
>
<details>
<summary><strong>Allowed Minecraft Modifications</strong></summary>

> - Cosmetic texture or resource packs.  
> - Fair-play client-side quality-of-life tools that do not affect gameplay balance (e.g., minimaps, world maps, keybind managers).  
> - Performance or visual enhancements that provide no gameplay advantage. An exception is granted for mods or resource packs that grant night vision or high gamma. 
</details>

### 6. Mental Disorder Roleplay
> - The roleplaying of documented mental disorders is strictly forbidden. This includes, but is not limited to, affective disorders, anxiety disorders, and psychotic disorders.
>   
> - Prohibited behavior includes, but is not limited to:  
>   - Roleplaying self-harm, suicide, or encouragement of self-harm.  
>   - Roleplaying hallucinations, psychotic episodes, or other severe symptoms. 
>   - Mocking, trivializing, or misrepresenting mental disorders in any way.
