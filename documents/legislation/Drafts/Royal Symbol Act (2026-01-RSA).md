## Chapter 1 ― General Provisions

### § 1. Purpose and scope
**1.** The purpose of this Act is to establish national symbols recognized by the Government and protect such national symbols.

**2.** This Act shall have jurisdiction over all territories of the Kingdom of Zatsunei upon becoming law.  

### § 2. Definitions
**1.** For the purposes of this Act, the term "national symbols" refers to any emblems, flags, anthems, or other acoustic, written, or visual expressions that are recognized by the Government as national symbols.

### § 3. Recognition of national symbols
**1.** The formal recognition of a national symbol is granted through an amendment of this Act or the enactment of a law by the Government that recognizes such a symbol as a national symbol.
